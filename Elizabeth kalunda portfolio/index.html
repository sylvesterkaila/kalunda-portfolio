About Me

CPA Prof Elizabeth Kalunda is a council member and the Vice Chair of the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Kenya (ICPAK). She is a qualified accountant with over 26 years of experience in Finance and accounting from academia and Industry. She is a passionate advocate for women in accounting and finance, and she is committed to promoting diversity and inclusion in the profession. She served as the Acting Deputy Vice Chancellor Finance and Administration United States International University Africa (USIU-A).

CPA. Prof. Kalunda is a frequent speaker and writer on accounting and finance topics. She has been featured in a number of media outlets, including the Business Daily and the Business Monthly, East African Edition. She is a co author of a book titled. 'We Took The Risk'.

CPA. Prof. Elizabeth Kalunda's academic journey reflects her dedication to the profession and knowledge. She earned a Doctorate in Business Administration with a specialization in Banking from the University of Nairobi, building upon her Master's in Business Administration (Accounting) from the same institution. A Certified Public Accountant (CPA-K) since 2003, she also holds a Bachelor of Education from Moi University. She is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA-K) and a Certified Public Mediator (CPM). She also lends her expertise as a representative of ICPAK to the boards of the Kenya Accountants and Secretaries National Examinations Board (Kasneb) where she is serving her second and final term.

Mission & Vision

Guiding Principles for My Journey

My Mission

To serve the accounting profession with integrity, passion, and purpose—advancing ethical leadership, strengthening regulatory frameworks, and promoting lifelong learning. Prof. Kalunda is dedicated to enhancing member value, driving impactful policy reforms, nurturing the next generation of professionals, and fostering a united, inclusive, and future-ready accounting community.

My Vision

To empower a dynamic and ethical accounting profession that drives national development, inspires global standards, and uplifts every member through inclusive leadership, innovation, and unwavering integrity.

My Manifesto

My commitment to the membership under the tagline “Dedicated Service: Amplified Voice of ICPAK is anchored on the SLEEK 2.0 agenda which emphasizes on five core pillars derived from the Strategic Plan (2025-29). This agenda places ICPAK membership at the heart of every initiative, ensuring that we uplift the profession, empower accountants while upholding public trust in an environment where Integrity Counts.

S

Setting the Bar in Standards for Superior Service

Prof. Elizabeth Kalunda is committed to four strategic pillars: 1. Standards Excellence, 2. Superiority in Practice, 3. Streamlined Membership and Licensing, and 4. 360 Degrees Sustainability — all aimed at elevating the accounting profession.

L

Leading Member-centric Institute

Prof. Elizabeth Kalunda also focuses on strengthening member value through 1. Innovative technologies to lift satisfaction and engagement, 2. Member Benefit Programs, 3. Leveraging the CPA brand for greater influence, and 4. Community outreach and pro bono services to extend professional impact.

E

Empowerment of Upcoming Accountants & Inclusivity

Prof. Kalunda is equally passionate about advancing professional growth through 1. Mentorship and career development, 2. Value-driven networking opportunities, 3. A strong youth empowerment agenda, and 4. Championing inclusivity for accountants with disabilities, ensuring no one is left behind.

E

Empowered Profession for Enhanced Public Interest

Prof. Kalunda remains committed to advancing the profession by 1. Elevating public awareness, 2. Strengthening stakeholder relationships, 3. Promoting respect, protection, and fair remuneration underpinned by ethics and compliance, and 4. Enhancing brand visibility while empowering devolution through ICPAK branches.

K

Knowledge Generation & Innovation

Prof. Kalunda continues to shape the future of the accounting profession through 1. Knowledge and market-driven research initiatives, 2. Expanding access to knowledge and insights, 3. Driving innovation with AI-enhanced technologies, and 4. Strengthening collaborations in research and thought leadership.

2.0

Amplified Voice of ICPAK

Prof. Kalunda’s leadership is grounded in 1. Unwavering integrity, 2. Strong protection of member interests, 3. Bold, transparent dialogue, and 4. Amplifying the voices of professionals across the accounting landscape.
